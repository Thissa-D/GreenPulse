Introduction to Climate Action

Climate action means taking steps to reduce climate change and protect the Earth.

Causes of Climate Change

Burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and pollution increase greenhouse gases.

Effects on the World

Climate change can cause heatwaves, floods, droughts, and sea-level rise.

Simple Solutions

We can save energy, plant trees, reduce waste, and use eco-friendly transport.
